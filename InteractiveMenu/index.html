Mac and Cheese

Macaroni and cheese is a popular, rich, and creamy pasta dish made of macaroni pasta and a cheese sauce, often cheddar, served in various forms from stovetop to baked casseroles.

Veg Pizza

A flatbread topped with tangy sauce, cheese, and fresh veggies like onions, capsicum, and mushrooms.

Burger

A juicy patty inside a bun with lettuce, tomato, onions, and sauces for a perfect snack.

Pani Puri

Crispy puris filled with spicy water, potatoes, and chutneys, a street-food favorite in India.

Samosa Chaat

A delightful Indian street food where crispy samosas are topped with spicy chickpeas, yogurt, and a variety of tangy chutneys.

Biryani

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, meat (such as chicken, mutton, or beef), and a blend of spices, often including saffron, cooked together to create a rich and satisfying meal.

Masala Dosa

Masala dosa is a popular South Indian dish consisting of a crispy, thin crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter, filled with a spiced potato mixture. It's typically served with coconut chutney and sambar, a lentil-based vegetable stew.

Daal Makhani

Daal Makhani is a rich and creamy North Indian lentil dish made with black lentils and kidney beans, slow-cooked with butter and cream, and flavored with aromatic spices.

Shahi Paneer

Shahi Paneer is a rich and creamy curry made with paneer cooked in a luxurious gravy of yogurt, cream, nuts, and aromatic spices. It's a perfect accompaniment to naan or rice.

Butter Naan

Butter Naan is a soft, leavened flatbread served in India and Pakistan, typically enjoyed with kababs, lentils, and curries. Brushed with butter, it’s a restaurant favorite.
